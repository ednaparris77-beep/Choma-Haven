About Choma Haven

Choma Haven brings you the real taste of Kenyan nyama choma — juicy cuts grilled over open charcoal, served hot with ugali, fresh kachumbari, and ice-cold drinks. A place for friends, family, laughter, and good food. Inspired by the best traditions of Nairobi's grill spots. Come hungry, leave happy!
